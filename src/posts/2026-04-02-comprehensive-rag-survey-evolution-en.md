# Comprehensive RAG Survey: Evolution, Current Landscape, Future

**Paper:** A Comprehensive Survey of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Evolution, Current Landscape and Future Directions
**Authors:** Shailja Gupta, Rajesh Ranjan (CMU), Surya Narayan Singh
**arXiv:** [2410.12837](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.12837)
**Venue:** arXiv preprint (October 2024)

---

## RAG Evolution

1. **Phase 1 (2020):** RAG (Lewis et al.), REALM — end-to-end retriever + generator training
2. **Phase 2:** Sparse → Dense → Hybrid retrieval; learnable retrievers with generation feedback
3. **Phase 3 (Current):** Self-RAG (self-judging retrieval need), CRAG (auto-quality assessment), Adaptive RAG (dynamic strategy selection based on query complexity)

## Key Challenges

Scalability at billion-document scale, bias propagation from retrieved documents, privacy concerns, incomplete hallucination elimination.
