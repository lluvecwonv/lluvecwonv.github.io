# RAG for AI-Generated Content: A Survey

**Paper:** A Survey on Retrieval-Augmented Generation for AI-Generated Content
**arXiv:** [2402.19473](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.19473)
**Venue:** arXiv preprint (February 2024)

---

## Scope

RAG applied to seven AIGC modalities: text, code, images, audio, video, 3D, and science. Addresses hallucination, knowledge staleness, and domain-specific knowledge gaps.

## Modality-Specific Applications

- **Text:** QA, summarization, dialogue (most mature)
- **Code:** Snippet retrieval, API documentation, structural vs. functional similarity
- **Image/Video/Audio:** Reference-guided generation (early stage)
- **Science:** Molecular design, protein prediction, math problem solving

## Key Challenges

Noise injection from poor retrieval, real-time retrieval at scale, cross-modal RAG integration, standardized benchmarks.
