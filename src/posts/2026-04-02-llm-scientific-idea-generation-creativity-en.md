# LLMs for Scientific Idea Generation: A Creativity-Centered Survey

**Paper:** Large Language Models for Scientific Idea Generation: A Creativity-Centered Survey
**arXiv:** [2511.07448](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07448)
**Venue:** arXiv preprint (November 2025)

---

## Creativity Frameworks Applied

- **Boden Taxonomy:** Exploratory (new combinations within existing space), Combinational (connecting different domains), Transformational (redefining the conceptual space itself)
- **Rhodes 4Ps:** Person (model characteristics), Process (prompting/reasoning strategies), Product (novelty/validity), Press (evaluation criteria)

## Five Method Families

1. **External Knowledge Augmentation:** RAG, paper database retrieval
2. **Prompt-based Distributional Steering:** Prompt engineering for creative outputs
3. **Inference-time Scaling:** More compute at inference (CoT, tree search)
4. **Multi-agent Collaboration:** Critic, proposer, verifier roles
5. **Parameter-level Adaptation:** Fine-tuning, RLHF for scientific creativity

## Key Insight

Most LLM "creativity" remains at the exploratory/combinational level. True transformational creativity is rare. The novelty-validity tradeoff is the central tension across all methods.
